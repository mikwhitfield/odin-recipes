Pea & Ham Soup

Home

Pea and ham soup is a hearty and comforting dish made with split peas, ham, and vegetables. It is a popular dish in many cultures and is often enjoyed during the colder months.

Ingredients

  • 250g split peas, rinsed
  • 200g ham hock or diced ham
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 liter chicken or vegetable stock
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine the split peas, ham, onion, carrots, celery, and garlic.
  2. Add the stock and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 1-2 hours until the peas are soft.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Blend the soup until smooth if desired.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.
