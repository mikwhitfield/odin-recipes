chilli con carne

Home

chilli con carne is a spicy stew containing chili peppers, meat (usually beef), and often tomatoes and beans.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, cooking until softened.
  3. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add the kidney beans and diced tomatoes, bringing to a simmer.
  5. Cook for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with rice, cornbread, or tortilla chips. Top with shredded cheese, sour cream, or avocado if desired.

Nutrition Information

Per serving: approximately 350 calories, 25g protein, 20g fat, 15g carbohydrates.

Conclusion

Chilli con carne is a hearty and flavorful dish that is perfect for a cozy dinner. Enjoy!
